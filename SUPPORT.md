## Frequently Asked Questions

### Account

**How do I delete my account?**
Go to **Profile → Delete Account** in the app. This permanently removes your account, training profile, and locally stored session data. Note: deleting your account does **not** cancel an active Apple subscription — you must cancel that separately in **iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions**.

**I forgot my password / can't sign in.**
If you signed in with Apple or Google, use the respective provider's account recovery flow. For email sign-in, tap **Forgot Password** on the login screen to receive a reset link.

---

### Subscriptions & Billing

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to **iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions**, find Kinetic, and tap **Cancel Subscription**. All billing is handled by Apple; we cannot cancel or refund subscriptions directly.

**How do I get a refund?**
Refunds are processed by Apple. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) and select the Kinetic purchase you'd like to dispute.

**Why did my analysis quota reset?**
Your quota resets at the start of each billing period (weekly or monthly, depending on your plan). The remaining count is shown on the Home screen.

### AI Coaching

**Why does coaching sometimes give inaccurate feedback?**
Coaching is powered by AI pose estimation and may be affected by camera angle, lighting, clothing, or algorithm limitations. Results are approximations — see our full disclaimer in the [Terms of Service](./TERMS.md#8-ai-coaching--important-disclaimer).

**Is my video uploaded to the cloud?**
No. Videos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded to Kinetic's servers. Only three still frames and a structured summary are sent when you explicitly tap **Get AI Coaching**. See our [Privacy Policy](./PRIVACY.md) for details.
